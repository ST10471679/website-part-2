ABOUT US


IFALIKA MTHETHWA (Pty) Ltd is a Level 1 BBBEE-certified company based in Mpumalanga, proudly serving the
construction and plant hire industry since 2018. We specialize in providing reliable machinery and equipment
for projects ranging from home building and civil works to general construction and site development. As a
100% black-owned business we are committed to quality service, safety and empowering local communities
through job creation and professional support. Whether you're undertaking a large-scale development or a
smaller residential project, IFALIKA MTHETHWA is equiped to meet your needs with efficiency and expertise.
IFALIKA MTHETHWA is initially serving clients within the Mbombela Municipality.
Over the years, the business has expanded its operations
to White River and Witbank, offering high-quality services with a focus on safety, efficiency and customer
satisfaction. The company has earned a strong reputation for delivering reliable solutions backed by skilled
professionals and a commitment to continuous improvement.

IFALIKA MTHETHWA has successfully worked with Norse Projects (Pty) Ltd, further demonstrating its
capability to handle projects for reputable clients while maintaining high standards of professionalism and
excellence.

OUR MISSION

To provide dependable and high-quality yellow plant hire services across Mpumalanga.

To deliver well-maintained equipment such as TLBs and excavators.

To offer professional service supported by skilled operators.

To prioritize safety, reliability and efficiency in every project.

OUR VISION

To become a leading plant hire company in South Africa.

To be known for excellence, reliability and innovation in the construction equipment sector.

To expand our footprint across provinces offering modern machinery and tailored solutions.

To continuously improve our services and invest in the latest technology.
